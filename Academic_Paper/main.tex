\begin{abstract}
This document serves as a standard template for writing academic papers. It features a two-column layout, robust author affiliation formatting, and standard packages typically required for scientific typesetting. The abstract should clearly state the problem, the proposed methodology, the core experiments, and the primary conclusions. It must be concise, usually between 150 to 250 words, and should not contain citations or complex mathematical expressions.
\end{abstract}
