\subsection{Mathematical Formulation}
Let $\mathcal{D} = \{(\mathbf{x}_i, y_i)\}_{i=1}^N$ be a dataset of $N$ samples. We optimize the objective function defined in Equation \ref{eq:objective}:

\begin{equation}
    \mathcal{J}(\theta) = - \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^N L(y_i, f(\mathbf{x}_i; \theta)) + \lambda \Omega(\theta)
    \label{eq:objective}
\end{equation}

where $L$ is the loss function, $f$ is the predictive model parameterized by $\theta$, and $\Omega(\theta)$ is a regularization term controlled by hyperparameter $\lambda$.
